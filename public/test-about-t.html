- About Us -



JS-SINTAX was created by a small group of fullstack bootcamp students from the University of Utah wanting to find out the best way to learn JavaScript. They thought about the common difficulties in quickly learning and absorbing JavaScript as a programming language and what aspects they were the weakest in with their technical knowledge of the language. After being on the same page of what was the hardest information to retain, they realized it was the termimonlogy of the syntax of JavaScript, and JS-SYNTAX was born. Our plan was to make a fun and simple way to learn and retain the syntax of JavaScript so that students of the language can have that extra edge in technical knowledge.
